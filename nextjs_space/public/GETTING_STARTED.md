## 2. Dashboard Overview

Your **Dashboard** is the heart of CashFlowIQ. Here's what you'll see:

### Key Metrics Cards

**💰 Current Cash Balance**
- Shows your available cash right now
- Updates as you add transactions

**📈 30-Day Cash Flow**
- Projected cash flow for the next 30 days
- Positive = more money coming in
- Negative = more money going out

**🔥 Monthly Burn Rate**
- Average amount you spend each month
- Helps you understand your expenses

**📅 Runway**
- Months of operations you can sustain
- Based on current burn rate

### Cash Flow Chart
- Shows your **90-day forecast** with a line graph
- Blue line = projected cash over time
- Helps you spot potential cash shortfalls

### Upcoming Transactions
- Lists transactions coming in the next few days
- Quick action buttons to add or edit

## 3. Adding Transactions

### Manual Transaction Entry (5 minutes)

You can manually add income and expenses:

**Step 1**: Click **"+ Add Transaction"** button (on Dashboard or Transactions page)

**Step 2**: Fill in the form:

- **Date**: When the transaction occurred
- **Description**: What is this transaction? (e.g., "Client Invoice", "Office Rent")
- **Type**: 
  - **Income** = Money coming in (sales, payments, refunds)
  - **Expense** = Money going out (rent, supplies, payroll)
- **Category**: Choose a category for better organization:
  - *Income categories*: Client Payment, Refund, Bonus, Other Income
  - *Expense categories*: Rent, Utilities, Payroll, Supplies, Equipment, Marketing, Professional Fees, Other Expense
- **Amount**: Dollar amount (e.g., 1500.00)

**Step 3**: Click **"Add Transaction"**

✅ Done! Your dashboard will update automatically.

### Editing or Deleting Transactions

1. Go to **Transactions** in the sidebar
2. Find the transaction you want to change
3. Click the **edit icon (pencil)** to modify
4. Click the **delete icon (trash)** to remove

## 4. Connecting Your Bank Account ⭐

### Why Connect Your Bank?

✅ **Automatic synchronization** — transactions import automatically  
✅ **Real-time data** — always up-to-date balances  
✅ **Accuracy** — no manual data entry errors  
✅ **Time-saving** — forget about manual entry  

### Step-by-Step Bank Connection

**Step 1**: Click **"Settings"** in the sidebar (gear icon)

**Step 2**: Scroll to **"Connected Bank Accounts"** section

**Step 3**: Click **"Connect Your Bank"** button

**Step 4**: A window will open (Plaid Link interface):
- Select your bank from the list (search or scroll)
- Enter your online banking credentials
  - *Example: Your Chase, Bank of America, Wells Fargo username & password*
  - **Your credentials are secure** — Plaid is used by thousands of apps

**Step 5**: Select which accounts to connect:
- You can choose Checking, Savings, or both
- Only accounts you select will sync

**Step 6**: Click **"Connect"**

✅ **Done!** Your bank account is now connected.

### What Happens Next?

1. **Automatic Sync**: Transactions sync automatically (usually within 24 hours)
2. **Manual Sync**: You can click **"Sync Now"** to refresh immediately
3. **Auto-Categorization**: The app automatically categorizes transactions
4. **Updates**: New transactions appear on your Dashboard and Transactions page

### Viewing Connected Accounts

In **Settings** under "Connected Bank Accounts":
- See all your linked banks
- Check last sync time
- Manually sync by clicking **"Sync Now"**
- Remove an account with **"Disconnect"** button

## 5. Understanding Forecasts

### What is a Forecast?

A **forecast** predicts your future cash position based on:
- Your past transaction patterns
- Current cash balance
- Upcoming transactions you've entered

### Reading the Chart

**On your Dashboard**:
1. Find the **"90-Day Cash Flow Forecast"** chart
2. The **blue line** shows projected cash over time
3. **Going up** = surplus (positive cash flow)
4. **Going down** = deficit (cash going out faster than coming in)

### What the Numbers Mean

- **Current Cash Balance**: Your cash today
- **30-Day Cash Flow**: Total projected change in next 30 days
- **Burn Rate**: Average monthly expenses
- **Runway**: How many months you can operate with current cash

### Example
If your **Runway = 6 months**, it means with your current cash and burn rate, you have **6 months before running out of money** (if nothing changes).

## 7. Viewing Reports

### Accessing Reports

1. Click **"Reports"** in the sidebar
2. You'll see three key reports:

### Report Types

**📊 Income**
- Total money coming in (last 90 days)
- Shows revenue trends

**📉 Expenses**
- Total money going out (last 90 days)
- Shows spending trends

**💵 Net Cash Flow**
- Income minus Expenses
- Shows whether you're profitable

### Charts

**Category Breakdown (Pie Chart)**
- Shows which categories you spend/earn the most in
- Click a slice to see details

**Monthly Comparison (Bar Chart)**
- Compares income vs. expenses each month

### Using Reports

- 📈 **Identify trends** — Are expenses increasing?
- 💡 **Find opportunities** — Which categories to cut?
- 📊 **Share with investors** — Show business health
- 🎯 **Set goals** — "I want to reduce expenses by X%"

## 9. Getting Help

### In-App Support

1. Click **"Support"** in the sidebar
2. Send us a message with your question
3. We'll respond within 24 hours

### Ask Sage

- If you have questions about **your data**, ask Sage!
- Sage can explain your transactions, forecasts, and trends

### Common Issues

#### "My bank account won't connect"
- Ensure your banking credentials are correct
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting
- Some banks require special setup (call your bank)

#### "Transactions aren't showing up"
- Check **"Last Synced At"** in Settings
- Click **"Sync Now"** to force a refresh
- Allow 24 hours for initial sync

#### "I forgot my password"
- Click **"Forgot Password"** on login page
- Check your email for reset link
- Or use **"Sign in with Google"** instead

#### "I want to change my email"
- Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
- Currently, email is not editable (contact Support)
